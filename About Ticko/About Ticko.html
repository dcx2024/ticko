About Ticko

What is TICKO?

TICKO is a smart online ticketing platform made for Nigerians, by poeple who've gone through those frustrating circumstances. With TICKO, you can create events, sell tickets, get paid, and checks in guests without breaking a sweat. Whether its Church Programs, Concerts, Conferences, Weddings. If it Brings People together, Ticko can handle it.

Real Talk

"You're probably thinking "this is just another startup, It might not last".Yeah we thought that too. But we aren't just all hype- we're building something solid, scalable and made to work for anyone. We're fixing what's broken in this space and giving every event, BIG or small, a platform to shine."

Our Mission

Pretty Simple. To create cheap and quality ticketing for both event explorers and organizers so they can create unforgettable memories

Why It Matters

Events should be simple for everyone(Organizers and guests). Ticko takes out the stress so everyone can enjoy the vibes.

Join The Movement

Whether it's your first event or you're just making memories with loved ones, TICKO's got you. So go ahead - Skip the line, Join the vibes.
